About Me

I am a passionate forensic science researcher currently working as a Research Assistant at the National Forensic Service of South Korea. My work focuses on developing cutting-edge AI-based methods for fingerprint enhancement and forensic imaging applications.

With a Master's degree in Forensic Science from Sungkyunkwan University, I specialize in combining traditional forensic techniques with modern AI technologies, particularly deep learning models like VDSR for image enhancement.
